PB &J Recipe!

Learn how to make a simple Penut Butter and Jelly Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Fruit Jelly)

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Spead Jelly on the other slice.
  4. Put the two slices together
